# Domain Glossary

Terms, aliases, relationships, example dialogue. A glossary, not a spec.

## Relationships

- **Person → Church**: Many-to-one (a Person belongs to one Church at a time)
- **Church → Conference**: Many-to-one (a Church belongs to one Conference)
- **Conference → Union → Division → General Conference**: Many-to-one chain
- **Person → Church Office**: Many-to-many (a Person can hold multiple offices simultaneously)
- **Person → Ministry**: Many-to-many (a Person can participate in and lead multiple ministries)
- **Church → Ministry**: One-to-many (a Church has multiple ministries)
- **Person → YouthClub → Church**: Person belongs to YouthClub belonging to Church
- **Person → ClassCompletion**: One-to-many (tracks progression through youth classes)
- **Person → MemberGiving**: One-to-many (each member has many giving records)
- **MemberGiving → EnvelopeCount**: One-to-one (each self-report verified by one count)
- **Church → WeeklyFinancialSummary**: One-to-many (weekly records)
- **Church → SabbathSchoolClass**: One-to-many (multiple classes per church)
- **Church → CensusSession**: One-to-many (annual census per church)
- **Person → CensusConfirmation**: One-to-many (per census session)
- **Person → GuestRecord**: One-to-many (guest may visit multiple times)
- **Person → PastoralVisit**: One-to-many (pastor visits person multiple times)
- **Church → Announcement**: One-to-many (church has many announcements)
- **Church → Report**: One-to-many (many statistical reports over time)
- **Organizational Unit → Report**: One-to-many (aggregated from subordinates)
- **Organizational Unit → Directive**: One-to-many (a unit issues multiple directives)
- **Directive → Recipient Units**: One-to-many (a directive targets multiple units)
- **Conference → Person (Pastor)**: One-to-many (Conference appoints pastors)

## Example Dialogue

"When a **Person** completes **BaptismClass**, they are baptized at a **BaptismEvent** and added to the membership roll — the **Church Clerk** needs to do nothing; the system auto-generates the **Membership Report**. If the **Person** later moves, a **TransferRequest** is issued and both churches are notified. Each week, members self-report their **MemberGiving** by photographing their envelope. The **Treasurer** performs the **EnvelopeCount** on Saturday night, verifying each self-report and flagging discrepancies in the **WeeklyFinancialSummary**. **AnonymousOffering** (unnamed envelopes, loose cash) is tallied separately and distributed according to the **CombinedOfferingPlan**. Once a year, the **CensusSession** prompts members to self-confirm; the Clerk only follows up on unconfirmed. **SabbathSchoolClass** teachers record attendance weekly, which feeds into the **Sabbath School Report**. **YouthClubs** (Pathfinders, Adventurers) track member **ClassCompletion** through progressive classes. **Guests** enter the pipeline through **GuestRecord**, are followed up, and may progress to **BaptismClass** and eventually membership."
