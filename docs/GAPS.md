### Core grassroots gaps
- **Membership records** — baptisms, transfers, attendance, retention, lost or fragmented records.  
- **Financial transparency and remittances** — inconsistent receipts, late remittances, weak audit trails.  
- **Governance and board minutes** — missing agendas, incomplete minutes, unclear motions and roles.  
- **Ministry reporting** — AY, Sabbath School, PM, Health, Dorcas reporting is ad hoc and inconsistent.  
- **Officer training and onboarding** — no structured onboarding, no certification or renewal tracking.  
- **Communication and coordination** — reliance on WhatsApp and informal channels; no unified calendar.  
- **Data integrity** — spreadsheets and paper create fragmentation and loss.  




---

### Operational and programmatic gaps (expanded)
- **Asset and property management** — unclear ownership, missing maintenance and insurance records.  
- **Volunteer and HR tracking** — no central roster, skills database, or volunteer workload tracking.  
- **Child safety and compliance** — missing training records, renewal reminders, incident workflows.  
- **Event planning and resource coordination** — no booking, volunteer assignment, or budget tracking.  
- **Inventory and supplies management** — literature, Dorcas supplies, and equipment lack tracking.  
- **Transportation and logistics** — vehicle logs, fuel, maintenance, and travel claims are manual.  
- **Risk management and incident reporting** — no standardized incident forms or escalation paths.  
- **Document lifecycle and version control** — outdated forms, multiple versions, no central source.  
- **Attendance and engagement analytics** — no trend analysis, retention metrics, or dashboards.  
- **Pastoral reporting and district oversight** — inconsistent visit logs and church visit documentation.  
- **Budgeting and local financial planning** — no forecasting, variance tracking, or budget templates.  
- **Training curriculum delivery and certification** — no tracked curriculum, progress, or renewals.  
- **Conflict resolution and governance accountability** — no documented escalation or mediation workflows.

### Advanced structural and cross‑level gaps
- **Inter‑department coordination** — silos between Health, Education, PM, AY, SS, Dorcas.  
- **Policy compliance monitoring** — no checklists, dashboards, or automated reminders.  
- **Audit readiness and documentation** — missing evidence, incomplete archives, poor provenance.  
- **Cross‑church data consistency** — inconsistent formats, naming, and reporting cycles.  
- **Leadership succession planning** — no officer history, no succession pipelines.  
- **District strategic planning** — lack of multi‑year plans, KPIs, and forecasting.  
- **Mission and union data aggregation** — inconsistent local reporting prevents accurate aggregation.  
- **Discipleship and retention tracking** — baptisms without post‑baptism engagement tracking.  
- **Volunteer burnout and workload tracking** — no rotation planning or burnout indicators.  
- **Digital literacy and training gaps** — low familiarity with digital tools among officers.  
- **Emergency response coordination** — no disaster workflows, contact trees, or incident plans.  
- **Cross‑border membership movement** — slow or lost transfers across islands and countries.  
- **Language and translation gaps** — inconsistent translations and low‑literacy UX needs.  
- **Technology fragmentation** — WhatsApp, Facebook, Excel, paper, and USBs create a fractured ecosystem.

### Technical, legal, and adoption gaps
- **Funding and sustainability model** — unclear recurring funding, donor vs subscription models.  
- **Fundraising and donor management** — pledge tracking, restricted funds, donor receipts.  
- **Archival and retention policy** — legal and ecclesial retention rules not codified.  
- **Records migration and legacy import** — ETL, validation, provenance for spreadsheets/paper.  
- **Volunteer support and helpdesk** — tiered support, SLAs, local champions, training hubs.  
- **Change management and adoption incentives** — rollout playbooks and pilot incentives.  
- **Data privacy and sovereignty** — per‑country storage rules, consent capture, export controls.  
- **Encryption and key management** — at‑rest encryption, key rotation, recovery procedures.  
- **Offline sync conflict resolution** — deterministic merge rules and manual reconciliation UIs.  
- **Backup, disaster recovery, and archival** — RTO/RPO, automated exports, independent archives.  
- **Monitoring, observability, and incident response** — metrics, alerts, runbooks, on‑call.  
- **Interoperability and APIs** — documented APIs for unions and third‑party systems.  
- **Testing, QA, and release management** — staging, automated tests, migration testing.  
- **Regulatory compliance across jurisdictions** — tax, charitable registration, remittance rules.  
- **Child protection and consent management** — consent capture, background check retention.  
- **Data access governance** — approval workflows for sensitive exports and audit committees.  
- **Vendor lock‑in and exit strategy** — exportable formats and migration plans off Cloudflare.  
- **Ethical use and policy guardrails** — limits on automation and misuse prevention.  
- **Language localization and literacy design** — multi‑language UI and low‑literacy flows.  
- **Cultural sensitivity and contextual workflows** — local titles, customs, and labels.  
- **Leadership buy‑in and governance of Theobase** — steering committee and data stewardship roles.  
- **Community feedback and product governance** — structured feedback loops and transparency.
